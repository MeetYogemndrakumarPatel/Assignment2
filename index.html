Virat Kohli: Born to Inspire

Virat Kohli was born on 5 November 1988 Dehli, INDIA. "Self-belief and hard work will always earn you success. "

Achievements

Over his lifetime, **Virat Kohli** achieved significant milestones such as becoming the fastest batsman to score 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in ODIs, leading India to historic Test series victories in Australia and England, scoring the highest runs in a single IPL season (973 in 2016), and receiving prestigious honors like the ICC Cricketer of the Year award (multiple times) and the Padma Shri in 2017.

Category Details
Profession Professional Cricketer
Role Right-handed batsman, occasional bowler
International Debut ODI: August 18, 2008 (vs. Sri Lanka)
Notable Records 973 runs in a single IPL season (2016), 80+ International Centuries and many more
Achievements Fastest to 10,000 ODI runs, ICC Cricketer of the Year 2017 & 2018
National Awards Padma Shri (2017), Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna (2018)
Hobbies Fitness, Traveling, Reading

Legacy

The contributions of **Virat Kohli** continue to inspire generations worldwide, showcasing the importance of hard work, discipline, and resilience. His achievements on the cricket field and his commitment to fitness and philanthropy have made him a role model for aspiring athletes and fans globally.
